About Coyote

According to the Washington Department of Fish and Wildlife In pioneer days, coyotes were restricted primarily to the sagebrush lands, brushy mountains, and open prairies of the American West. Coyotes have since taken advantage of human activities to expand their range throughout North and Central America. The coyote's tenacity tries some people's patience and inspires others' admiration.
